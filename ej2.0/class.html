Foundatios of Aerospace

Attendees will gain an understanding of the fundamentals of aeronautics and astronautics, as well as an appreciation for the interdependence of key space mission elements.

Intro to foundations of Aerospace

Describe NASA’s contributions to the aeronautics industry.

Intro to Aerospace Engineering

Explain the relationship between basic aircraft functions and aircraft design.

How does a rocket work?

Describe basic aircraft functions including aerodynamics, stability and control, structures, propulsion, and performance.

Fundamentals of System Engineering

Systems engineering focuses on the interfaces among the people, processes, and products that are often outside the responsibility of any one function or discipline. This course equips your teams with the knowledge necessary to realize successful solutions. This course introduces the methods and techniques for a structured systems development process that proceeds from requirements to concept to production to operation, and is based upon NASA policy guidelines, specifically NASA Procedural Requirements (NPR) 7123.1 and 7120.5.

Intro to Fundamentals of System Engineering

Calculate basic orbit and system parameters that drive mission scope and cost.

What is "Systems Engineering"?

Define and describe important astronautics terms and concepts including those related to the space environment; orbit description, prediction and maneuvering; and payload and spacecraft design.

Guest Talk: Day in the life of a Systems Engineering: Steve Smith

Explain the process for system requirements development and management./p>

International Project Management

This course provides project practitioners with an understanding of cultural challenges, legal concerns, and teaming issues that are likely to be encountered when working with international partners. Two distinct facets of successful international project management are addressed: technical knowledge and cultural understanding. Participants gain insights into the characteristics of international teaming that have the potential to make or break a project through the use of lectures, small group discussion, hands-on practical exercises, and case studies.

Intro to international Project Management

Recognize and appreciate the project management approaches of the European Space Agency (ESA), Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA), and NASA as well as several other space agencies and companies who support them.

What is Project Management?

Apply behaviors associated with the “softer” side of cross-cultural relations needed to be effective in the International Project Management arena.

What Does a Project Manager Do

Analyze product and project risk and mitigation based upon NASA policies and practices.
